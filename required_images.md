# GreenBasket Image Generation Prompts Catalog

This document lists the required images for all 24 categories and 240 products. All images must be saved under the target paths in the `/public` folder of the storefront.

### Image Generation Guidelines:
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 square ratio (e.g., `512x512` or `1024x1024`).
- **Background**: Pure white, light grey, or pastel-cream background as specified per category.
- **Style**: Clean, bright, commercial product studio photography.
